# Scene 1 — Maya (problem felt)

## Beats
Maya in litter-strewn autumn park. Expression of quiet disappointment as she takes in the mess. Phone in hand but attention is on the ground around her — not yet on screen. The litter is foreground/midground, not background. This shot establishes the problem viscerally before any "tap to act" beat.
